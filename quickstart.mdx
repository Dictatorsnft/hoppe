## Creating Your First Arbitrage Position

Learn how to start arbitraging your funds on Hoppe.

### Setup Your Hoppe Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Get Hoppe Subscription">
    To onboard, you would first need to join Hoppe Discord Server.
    You would need ```Basic```, ```Plus``` or ```Beta Tester``` role in our server in order to proceed. To get these roles, follow the
    ```instructions``` provided in our server.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="rectangle-terminal" title="Login To Hoppe App">
    Login to your account on Hoppe App with your Approved Discord Account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Fund Your Hoppe Account

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="message-bot" title="Purchase USDC ">
  Hoppe operates on a stable asset 'USDC' to ensure consistent profitability amidst the unpredictable crypto markets. To get started, either acquire USDC from a CEX and transfer it to your Defi Wallet, or swap your ETH, SOL, or BNB to USDC within your Defi wallet.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Transfer USDC to Hoppe Account">
Once you are inside Hoppe App, click next to your username in top bar to connect your Defi wallet to our app. Once connected you would need to deposit USDC to your account in order to fund your arbitrage position in next step.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
