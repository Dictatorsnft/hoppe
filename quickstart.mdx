## Creating Your First Arbitrage Position

Learn how to start arbitraging your funds on Hoppe.

### Setup Your Hoppe Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Get Hoppe Subscription">
    To onboard, you would first need to join Hoppe Discord Server.
    You would need ```Basic```, ```Plus``` or ```Beta Tester``` role in our server in order to proceed. To get these roles, follow the
    ```instructions``` provided in our server.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="rectangle-terminal" title="Login To Hoppe App">
    Login to your account on Hoppe App with your Approved Discord Account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Fund Your Hoppe Account

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="coins" title="Purchase USDC ">
  Hoppe operates on a stable asset ```USDC``` to ensure consistent profitability amidst the unpredictable crypto markets. To get started, either acquire USDC from a CEX and transfer it to your Defi Wallet, or swap your ETH, SOL, or BNB to USDC within your Defi wallet.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Transfer USDC to Hoppe Account">
Once you are inside Hoppe App, click next to your username in top bar to connect your Defi wallet to our app. Once connected you would need to deposit ```USDC``` to your account in order to fund your arbitrage position in next step.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

### Open Arbitrage Position

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="coins" title="Select Arbitrage Type">
  Go to Arbitrage page and choose between ```Daily``` and ```Compound``` Arbitrage, depending on your investment strategy and preferred lock-up period.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Make Your Investment">
Choose the amount you wish to allocate to your arbitrage position and verify the expected daily profits. Click ```Open Position``` to open your arbitrage position.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>


## Managing Your Positions

### Monitor Your Investment

Once your investment is in place, you'll see an overview of your investment performance on your dashboard.

### Inspect Daily Profit

Enjoy the flexibility of withdrawing your profits whenever you like depending upon to the type of Arbitrage you choose. Simply initiate a withdrawal request, and your funds will be transferred to your connected wallet.

### Make Your Withdrawals

Your profits will be automatically calculated and credited to your account daily. Keep an eye on your growing balance.

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion icon="coins" title="Basic Arbitrage">
Your ```fixed profits``` are sent back to your account on daily bases.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Compound Arbitrage">
If you've selected Compound Arbitrage, your investment will be locked inside the bot and would automatically increase on daily bases as profits are reinvested during the lock-up period hence providing ```profit incrementation``` and will be withdrawn to your account once you have reached position expiration date.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
